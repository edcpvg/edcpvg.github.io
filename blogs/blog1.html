EDC PVGCOET is a six-year-old inter-departmental college club now, and with so many years that have gone past, we have learned to master the skill of event management and helping people through their entrepreneurship journey. The E-Cell of our college is ranked amongst the top E-Cells for the E-Summit held each year at IIT Bombay and has been striving to reach new levels as we grow older and more experienced each year. From the year 2019, we commenced on the new journey of hosting our inter-college annual event called 'Venturers."

Venturers is now counted amongst the biggest college-hosted events held in Pune. Through Venturers, we have influenced the lives of over 5k students, helping them scale up their entrepreneurship journey over the past years, and this year we plan to touch even new heights by inviting best-in-class speakers, entrepreneurs, and standups to attract a huge youth crowd from all corners of the city and beyond.
We were able to achieve this only because of our previous renowned sponsors like PolyCab India, Red Carpet, NPAV, Jhakas Misal, and many more well reached brands, who supported us, through which we were able to invite some of the most renowned entrepreneurs of India as speakers like Tamanjit Bindra(co-founder, Kaagaz scanner), Rajat Dangi(co-founder Hapramp), and Rishiraj Kalita (investment associate) ,Huddle(The leading client portal solution for professional services firms ) to make our events successful.
